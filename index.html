Principal’s Message

At Evergreen International School, we understand that the early years of a child’s education are the most important. These years shape not only academic ability, but also confidence, curiosity, and character.

Our school follows the CBSE-aligned curriculum with a strong focus on foundational learning, conceptual understanding, and age-appropriate teaching practices. We believe that children learn best in an environment that is safe, caring, and encouraging. Therefore, we give equal importance to academics, physical activity, values, and emotional well-being.

Our teachers are committed to guiding each child with patience and dedication, while working closely with parents to support every learner’s individual growth. We aim to create a joyful learning experience that prepares children for future academic stages with confidence.

At Evergreen International School, we strive to nurture happy learners, responsible individuals, and lifelong students of learning.

Principal
Evergreen International School

Correspondent’s Message

Evergreen International School was established with a simple and sincere purpose: to provide young children with a strong, caring, and meaningful start to their educational journey.

We believe that education at the primary level should focus on understanding, values, and overall development rather than pressure or competition. Our school follows the CBSE framework and adopts teaching practices that respect each child’s pace of learning, curiosity, and individuality.

Along with academic learning, we give importance to moral values, discipline, physical well-being, and social skills. A supportive environment, dedicated teachers, and active parent involvement are the pillars of our institution.

Our commitment is to continuously improve our educational practices and facilities, ensuring that every child at Evergreen International School feels safe, valued, and encouraged to grow.

A Strong Foundation for Lifelong Learning

Early education lays the foundation for a child’s academic and personal development. We focus on building strong foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and communication, ensuring children are prepared for future academic stages.

Our curriculum follows the CBSE-aligned framework and is delivered in a structured yet flexible manner, respecting each child’s pace of learning.

Child-Centric and Concept-Based Teaching
  • Activity-based learning.
  • Conceptual understanding.
  • Age-appropriate teaching methods.
  • Learning through observation, interaction, and practice.
Safe, Caring, and Supportive Environment

A child’s emotional well-being is as important as academic growth. Evergreen International School provides a safe, disciplined, and nurturing environment where children feel comfortable, respected, and encouraged.

Our campus is designed to be child-friendly, with supervised classrooms and play areas that support both learning and physical development.

Qualified and Dedicated Teachers

Our teachers are trained, experienced, and committed to early childhood and primary education. They guide students with patience and care, ensuring individual attention and positive reinforcement.
