School Overview

Strong foundations for lifelong learning.

Evergreen International School provides quality primary education rooted in strong values and modern teaching practices, aligned with the CBSE curriculum framework. We believe the early years shape a child's entire future.

Our Vision

To nurture intellectual, physical, emotional, and moral development, enabling students to become responsible global citizens.

Our Mission

Deliver CBSE-aligned education with strong foundational learning, conceptual understanding, and an inclusive environment.

  • Activity-based, child-centric teaching methods
  • Dedicated and experienced teaching faculty
  • Safe, nature-inspired campus environment
  • Aligned with NEP 2020 guidelines

Academics

Curriculum designed to build strong foundations with NEP 2020 alignment and child-centric methodology.

Teaching Methodology

Activity-based learning using visual and audio teaching aids, interactive classroom techniques, and continuous assessment tailored to each child's pace.

Pre-Primary (Pre-KG and KG)

Play-based learning, phonics, basic numeracy, drawing, storytelling, and social skills that lay a joyful foundation for formal schooling.

Primary Classes (Grades 1 to 5)

English, Mathematics, Environmental Science, Social Studies, General Knowledge, Computer Education, Art and Craft, and Physical Education.

Admissions

We welcome new students for the academic year 2025-26. Admissions are open for Pre-KG to Grade 5.

Admission Process

1
Enquiry and Interaction

Visit the school or call us. Speak with our admissions team and get a campus tour.

2
Application Form

Fill and submit the application form, available at the school office or via our enquiry form.

3
Document Verification

Submit the required documents. Our team will verify and confirm eligibility.

4
Admission Confirmed

Complete the fee process and receive your admission confirmation and class details.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (Original and Photocopy)
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Transfer Certificate for Grade 2 and above
  • Previous class mark sheet or report card
  • Parent or Guardian identity proof
  • Aadhaar card copy (optional)
School timings are 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. Contact us to schedule a personalised counselling session.
