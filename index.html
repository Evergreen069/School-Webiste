School Overview

Strong foundations for lifelong learning.

Evergreen International School provides quality primary education rooted in strong values and modern teaching practices, aligned with the CBSE curriculum framework. We believe the early years shape a child's entire future, and every decision we make reflects that belief.

Our Vision

To nurture intellectual, physical, emotional, and moral development, enabling students to become responsible and compassionate global citizens.

Our Mission

Deliver CBSE-aligned education with strong foundational learning, conceptual understanding, and a safe, inclusive environment for every child.

Why Parents Choose Evergreen

What sets us apart is not just what we teach, but how we teach it and who we are as a school community.

Child-Centric Care

Every child at Evergreen is known by name, understood as an individual, and supported at their own pace. We combine academic rigor with genuine warmth.

CBSE Aligned Curriculum

Our structured curriculum follows CBSE guidelines and NEP 2020 principles, ensuring students are well-prepared for every stage of their academic journey.

Experienced Teaching Team

Our qualified and dedicated teachers bring patience, creativity, and expertise to every classroom, making learning joyful and effective for all.

Safe and Secure Environment

From monitored entry to clean facilities and child-appropriate spaces, your child's safety and comfort is our highest priority every single day.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we nurture art, sports, music, and social skills, ensuring every child grows confidently in all aspects of their young life.

Strong Parent Partnership

Regular PTMs, open communication, and a responsive administration keep parents always informed and closely involved in their child's progress.

Academics

Our curriculum builds strong conceptual foundations, critical thinking, and a genuine love for learning from the very first day.

Teaching Methodology

Activity-based learning with visual and audio teaching aids, interactive sessions, hands-on projects, and continuous assessment tailored to each child's pace.

Pre-Primary (Pre-KG and KG)

Play-based learning, phonics, basic numeracy, drawing, storytelling, and social skills that lay a joyful and confident foundation for formal schooling.

Primary Classes (Grades 1 to 5)

English, Tamil, Mathematics, Environmental Science, Social Studies, General Knowledge, Computer Education, Art and Craft, and Physical Education.

Admissions

We welcome new students for the academic year 2025-26. Admissions are open for Pre-KG to Grade 5.

Admission Process

1
Enquiry and Interaction

Visit the school or call us. Speak with our admissions team and get a guided campus tour at your convenience.

2
Application Form

Fill and submit the application form, available at the school office or through the enquiry form on this page.

3
Document Verification

Submit the required documents. Our team will verify and confirm the child's eligibility for the applied grade.

4
Admission Confirmed

Complete the fee payment and receive your official admission confirmation along with class and timing details.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (Original and Photocopy)
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Transfer Certificate (for Grade 2 and above)
  • Parent or Guardian identity proof
  • Aadhaar card copy Mandatory
School timings are 7:45 AM to 5:30 PM, Monday to Saturday. Contact us to schedule a personalised counselling session before applying.

Infrastructure

A safe, modern, and child-friendly campus thoughtfully designed for joyful, distraction-free learning.

Spacious Classrooms

Well-ventilated, bright classrooms with age-appropriate furniture, clean boards, and all learning aids in place.

School Library

Curated age-appropriate books, storybooks, picture books, and reference materials for curious young readers.

Outdoor Play Area

Safe open grounds encouraging physical activity, team sports, and outdoor learning in a nature-friendly setting.

Smart Classrooms

Digital teaching aids and audio-visual tools that make every lesson engaging and memorable for young learners.

Health and Safety

Regular health check-ups, clean drinking water, proper sanitation, and a fully child-safe supervised campus.

Art and Activity Room

Secure Campus
