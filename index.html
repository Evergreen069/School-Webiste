School Overview

Strong foundations for lifelong learning.

Evergreen International School provides quality primary education rooted in strong values and modern teaching practices, aligned with the CBSE curriculum framework.

Our Vision

To provide holistic education that nurtures intellectual, physical, emotional, and moral development, enabling students to become responsible global citizens.

Our Mission
  • Deliver CBSE-aligned education with strong foundational learning.
  • Encourage conceptual understanding and critical thinking.
  • Create a safe, inclusive, and disciplined learning environment.
  • Foster values, creativity, and social responsibility.

Academics

Curriculum focused on foundational literacy and NEP 2020 alignment.

Teaching Methodology
  • Activity-based learning techniques.
  • Visual and audio teaching aids for better retention.
  • Interactive classroom practices.
  • Continuous assessment and observation.
Primary Classes (Grades 1-5)
  • English, Mathematics, Environmental Studies / Science.
  • Social Studies, General Knowledge.
  • Computer Education, Art and Craft.
  • Physical Education.

Admissions

Admissions are open for Pre-KG to Grade 5 based on seat availability.

Admission Process
  • Enquiry and interaction with school authorities.
  • Submission of application form.
  • Verification of documents.
  • Confirmation of admission.
Documents Required
  • Birth Certificate (Original & Copy).
  • Passport-size photographs.
  • Transfer Certificate (for Grades 2 and above).
  • Aadhaar copy (optional).

Infrastructure

A safe, hygienic, and child-friendly campus with essential facilities.

Facilities
  • Spacious and ventilated classrooms.
  • Library with age-appropriate books.
  • Outdoor play area.
  • Smart classroom support.
Health & Safety
  • Regular medical check-ups.
  • Child-safe campus environment.
  • Clean drinking water & sanitation.
